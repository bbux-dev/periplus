| direct URL navigation → ReviewPage | A bookmark/refresh hits the route with null state; must not crash |

| T-04-11 | Tampering | crafted entry data written to IndexedDB | accept | Dexie stores structured data; no eval/exec path. Single-user local store; no privilege boundary crossed. |
